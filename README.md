# ⚡ Energy Drink Market Analysis & Marketing Strategy (Code X)

📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of the energy drink market, combining consumer behavior, demographic insights, and marketing strategy recommendations.

🎯 Objectives

Analyze who consumes energy drinks and their behavior
Identify key drivers of purchase decisions
Evaluate market leaders & competition
Recommend data-driven marketing strategies

📊 Dashboard Highlights

👥 Demographics
60%+ consumers are male
Core audience: 15–30 age group
Younger audience heavily influenced by digital channels

💰 Price Sensitivity

Most preferred price range: ₹50–₹99 (~41.8%)
Ideal strategic pricing: ₹100–₹150

🛒 Purchase Behavior

Top purchase location: Supermarkets (44.9%)
Consumption occasions:
🏋️ Sports / Exercise (44.9%)
📚 Studying / Late work (32.3%)
🧪 Ingredient Preference

Most preferred:

⚡ Caffeine
💊 Vitamins
🌿 Guarana

📦 Packaging Insights

Consumers prefer:
Compact & portable cans (39.8%)
Innovative bottles (30.4%)

🏆 Market Leaders

Coca-Cola – 25.38%
Pepsi – 21.12%
Code X – ~9.8% (low penetration opportunity)

🧠 Key Insights

Brand reputation (26.5%) is the top purchase driver
Taste & flavor (20.1%) significantly impacts loyalty
Digital marketing dominates (the highest engagement channel)
The youth segment is the growth engine

**Code X suffers from:**
Weak branding
Limited distribution
Moderate taste perception
📢 Marketing Channel Effectiveness
Channel	Impact
🌐 Online Ads	Highest (4,020 responses)
📺 TV Commerce	Strong
🏢 Outdoor Ads	Moderate
📰 Print Media	Low

👉 Insight: Shift budget toward digital-first strategy

🔍 Competitive Analysis

Why competitors win:

Strong brand recall
Wide distribution network
Aggressive marketing
Consistent product experience
